Meet The Owner

Hi, I'm Diego Garcia

The owner and founder of Growl Food cafeteria. I'm passionate about providing delicious and nutritious food to the students and faculty of the University of Houston at Sugarland. I started Growl Food cafeteria in 2023, after graduating from the University of Houston with a degree in culinary arts. I wanted to create a place where I could showcase my skills and creativity, while also serving the community that I love. I named the cafeteria Growl Food, because I believe that food should make you feel good and satisfied, just like a growl.

Our Story:

How we became the best cafeteria in Sugarland

Our cafeteria was founded in 2023 by a group of students who wanted to create a place where they could enjoy good food and good company. We started with a small space and a limited menu, but we soon gained popularity among the students and faculty for our quality and service..
